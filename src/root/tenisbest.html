A Tour of Binary and Other Bases.

It is counting time, so let decimal show you how. Not so fast decimal! Binary is here to show kids there are other ways to count besides decimal. There’s unary, binary, base sixty, and more! Which one do you think is best? Find out what you gain, and what you lose, when you use bigger or smaller bases.

People use decimal numbers, while computers use binary. In computer science, it is vital that the same information can be written many different ways. You don't need to know binary to program, but it can help. However, without binary computers would be much, much slower.
